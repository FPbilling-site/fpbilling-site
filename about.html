Who We Are

Excellence in billing. Integrity in service. Results that scale.

With over 25 years of experience in healthcare revenue cycle management, FP Billing & Consulting Services has consistently helped home health agencies, clinics, and providers unlock their full financial potential. We specialize in HMO medical billing—a space where precision, compliance, and strategy matter most.

Our team is led by Felicia Ponder, a seasoned billing expert and strategic consultant, who has directly collaborated with managed care giants like NAMM, DaVita Healthcare Partners, Prospect Medical Group, and PIH Health Hospital. Under her leadership, FP Billing has helped clients grow from struggling operations into thriving practices—scaling some past $2 million+ in annual HMO billables.

What sets us apart? We're not just billers—we're partners. We help you identify bottlenecks, optimize workflows, and improve clean claim ratios. From pre-bill audits to denial management and advanced analytics, our systems are designed to recover lost revenue and accelerate reimbursements.

As a family-run business, we believe in treating every client like family—giving you white-glove service, total transparency, and responsive support. Our proven results are powered by a relentless focus on compliance, culture improvement, team engagement, and most importantly—your bottom line.

Whether you’re a startup agency or an established provider looking to scale, FP Billing delivers real results you can count on. Our clients don’t just get paid—they grow.
